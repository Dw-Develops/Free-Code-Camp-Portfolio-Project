Prize Wheel Web Page Plug In

The prize wheel application is designed to be a website plug in that will allow website owners to create an onsite currency for their products and or services and then allow users to gamble for better discounts on your website offerings. The idea is to gamify your business a bit to invite more intuative user interactions with your website.
